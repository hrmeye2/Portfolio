About Me

Hello! My name is Harley Meyers. I'm an undergraduate Audio/Music Production Major at Illinois State Univeristy. I am the founder of Harley Rose Studios, a new recording studio and safe space for women and queer artists. I've been producing for 4 years beginning with my first band HXI. I sang for 9 years, including at Carnegie Hall when I was 15 and I've played piano for 12 years. My goal is to craft beats and melodies to make your heart move and soul groove. Bringing music to life with every note I create.
